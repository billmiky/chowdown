tags:
- mains
- casserole

ingredients:
- Bag frozen broccoli
- Breasts of chicken
- 1/2 lb Velveeta cheese
- 1 can cream celery soup
- 1/2 cup mayo
- 1/2 cup sherry
- 1 can water chestnuts
- 1 can fried onion rings

 directions:
- In pan (greased): Broccoli, then meat; then mix the soup, sherry, cheese, & mayo & cook mix until cheese is melted.
- Pour on top, water chestnuts, cook 30 min, onion rings.
- Bake til browned at 350°.
