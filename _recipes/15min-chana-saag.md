---

title: "15 Minute Chana Saag"
image: 15min-chana-saag.png
tags: [Vegan, Indian, Curry, 15 Minutes, Gluten-Free]
ingredients:
- 180g baby spinach
- 1 handful fresh coriander (cilantro)
- 150g silken tofu
- 2 tbsp mango chutney
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 thumb fresh ginger, finely chopped
- 1 fresh red chili, finely chopped
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Salt, to taste
directions:
- Place spinach in a bowl, cover with boiling water, and blanch for 2 minutes. Drain and rinse immediately with cold water to cool.
- Blend the blanched spinach, coriander, silken tofu, and mango chutney until smooth. Set aside.
- Heat coconut oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, chili, garam masala, and turmeric; fry for a few minutes until fragrant.
- Add chickpeas and the green sauce to the pan. Rinse the blender with a splash of water and add to the pan.
- Simmer to heat through. Stir in lemon juice and salt. Serve with rice or bread.
---

A speedy, 15-minute vegan version of the classic Indian curry, Chana Saag (chickpeas and leafy greens). This recipe uses silken tofu for creaminess and mango chutney for a touch of sweetness.
